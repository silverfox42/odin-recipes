Steak Taco Recipe

Great as part of a larger meal, or as the star of the show, these steak tacos are a great blend of sweet and spicy flavors sure to wake up your sense.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb flank steak
  • Tortillas
  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • Fresh cilantro
  • 1 clove minced garlic
  • Queso Fresco
