Steak Taco Recipe

Great as part of a larger meal, or as the star of the show, these steak tacos are a great blend of sweet and spicy flavors sure to wake up your sense.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb flank steak
  • Tortillas
  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • Fresh cilantro
  • 1 clove minced garlic
  • Queso Fresco
  • 1 lime

Steps

  1. Marinate the steak for 1 hour in the pineapple juice, soy sauce and minced garlic.
  2. Sear the steak over medium-high heat for approximately 3 minutes on each side. Cook until medium-rare, using a meat thermometer if necessary.
  3. Take the steak off pan and allow to rest on a cutting board for at least 8 minutes.
  4. Cut the steak against the grain into slices 1/2 inch thick.
  5. Further cut each strip into cubes.
  6. Put the steak, diced cilantro, queso fresco, and lime juice onto the taco.
