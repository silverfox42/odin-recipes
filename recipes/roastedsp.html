Roasted Sweet Potato Recipe

This recipe is for roasted sweet potatoes. Modifications can be made to make this dish either sweet or savory. It is a great addition to almost any dinner, and a nice, warm addition to a fall meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs diced sweet potatoes
  • 2 olive oil
  • 2 tbsp salt and pepper
  • (Optional) pinch of cayenne powder and chili powder to taste
  • (Optional) 2 tbsp honey and cinnamon to taste

Steps

  1. Dice the sweet potatoes into small cubes.
  2. Place the cubes into a roasting pan.
  3. Add the olive oil, salt and pepper. Stir to coat evenly.
  4. Optional: add seasoning to make either sweet or savory.
  5. Roast at 425 degrees for 45 minutes.
  6. Remove from oven, allow to cool for 2 minutes and serve.
