Meatball Recipe

An Italian staple that pairs well with spaghetti or as an independent side dish, these meatballs will be sure to round out your Italian feast.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef (85% lean)
  • 1/2 medium yellow onion
  • 1 cup freshly shredded parmesan chesse
  • 1/2 cup Italian breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp salt and pepper
  • 1 clove minced garlic
